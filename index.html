About us

TalentNow is a talent-first recruitment company. We take the time to properly assess your skillset, and find you the perfect fit. Not the other way arond. We believe this is also in the best interest of the company you'll be placed at.

All of our recruiters have previously or are still working in the digital sector, either as developers, designers, project managers or product owners. After your application we'll match you with a recruiter with the most similar skillset. Someone who understands your position.

No Spam

We have a strict no harrasment policy. Once you apply and get trough the vetting process, we will ask you every once in a while about your availability. That's all.
